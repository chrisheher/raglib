Bernard has taken for his motto:

If not you, who will do it?
If not now, then when?

He tries to work this out in Latin. And when he is
thinking about getting Edouard’s suitcase out of the
checkroom: “If you don’t do it now, you run the risk
of letting Edouard do it.”

The charming thing about these maxims is that they
