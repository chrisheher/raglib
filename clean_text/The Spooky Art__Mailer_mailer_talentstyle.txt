No matter. With a talent as large as this, one could forgive the
obvious eye for success. Many a large talent has to go through
large borrowings in order to find his own style, and a lust for
popular success, while dangerous to serious writing, is not 
necessarily fatal. Yes, one could accept the pilferings from other
writers, given the scope of his work, the brilliance of the
