widely and quickly to otherwise healthy fowl. Thus the presumed
enemies of the grouse, by controlling the epizootic aspects of the
disease, proved to be friends in disguise.

In summarizing the above situation, the measure of validity
wouldn’t be to assume that, even in the well-understood factor of

condition, but to say, rather, that in this phase we have a highly
significant and possibly preponderantly important relational aspect
of the picture.
