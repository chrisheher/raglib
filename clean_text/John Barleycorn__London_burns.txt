And I laugh my defiance; for now, and for the moment, I know
the White Logic to be the arch-impostor of them all, whispering
his whispers of death. And he is guilty of his own unmasking,
with his own genial chemistry turning the tables on himself,
with his own maggots biting alive the old illusions, resurrecting
and making to sound again the old voice from beyond of my
youth, telling me again that still are mine the possibilities and
powers which life and the books had taught me did not exist.
