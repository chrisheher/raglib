clear and find an alternative route up or down the coast.

3. Wind and sea. Onshore winds and seas slam into an ebb current, creating
steep breaking seas over the entrance bar. Study the tidal current and
calculate the times of maximum ebb, maximum flood, and slack water. Wait
for slack current or the beginning of a flood before you attempt the passage.
