Appendix B
Spares

The extent of your spare parts inventory will
obviously depend on your cruising plans.
The following is a fairly comprehensive list
with an ocean cruising sailboat in mind. If
your plans are less ambitious it can be scaled
down appropriately.

1. Oil filters.

2. Fuel filters—quite a number in case a
dirty batch of fuel is taken on board and
repeated filter changes are needed.

3. An air filter—one only. This should
rarely need changing in the marine environment.

4. A raw-water pump overhaul kit, or at
the very least, a diaphragm or impeller
(depending on the type of pump).

5. A lift-pump overhaul kit, or at the very
least, a diaphragm.

6. A fuel injection pump diaphragm if one
is fitted.

7. A complete set of belts (alternator, plus
auxiliary equipment).

8. An alternator.

9. A starter motor solenoid and Bendix

unit.

. Two sets of valve springs and keepertil
