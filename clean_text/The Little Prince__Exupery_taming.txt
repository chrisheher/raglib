Only the man who was in charge of the single

When one wishes to play the wit, he sometimes
wanders a little from the truth. I have not been al-
together honest in what I have told you about the
lamplighters. And I realize that I run the risk of
giving a false idea of our planet to those who do
not know it. Men occupy a very small place upon
the Earth. If the two billion inhabitants who 
people its surface were all to stand upright 
and somewhat crowded together, as they do for some big
public assembly, they could easily be put into
one public square twenty miles long and twenty
miles wide. All humanity could be piled up on a
small Pacific islet.

The grown-ups, to be sure, will not believe you
when you tell them that. They imagine that they
fill a great deal of space. They fancy themselves
as important as the baobabs, You should advise
them, then, to make their own calculations. They
adore figures, and that will please them.
