This beautiful child, who had just begun to dance, 
and who stirred everyone who saw her then with the 
art of movement and transformation that was innate 
in her body and spirit—-declared unexpectedly to her 
mother that she no longer could or would dance
(this happened just at the end of childhood); 
her body changed, became, without losing its beautiful 
Slavic features, strangely heavy and massive... 
During the time that remained to her, Vera took up music; 
finally she only drew——, as if the denied dance
came forth from her ever more quietly, ever more discreetly.
