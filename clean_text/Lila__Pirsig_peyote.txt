After the sixties the whole issue of peyote became one of those
no-win political contests between individual freedom on the one hand
and democracy on the other. Clearly LSD was injuring some innocent
people with hallucinations that led to their death, and clearly the
majority of Americans wanted drugs such as LSD made illegal. But
the majority of Americans were not Indians and certainly they were
not members of the Native American Church. There was a persecution of 
a religious minority going on here, something that’s not
supposed to happen in America.
