ORIGIN OF INSPIRATION

in search of the way, of freedom, of understanding and inner
peace, and when I show them the safest ways to captain their
own ship to salvation, they almost always end up surrendering
their souls to others who promise them a magic pill. Magical
wonders of life are felt, not seen.

Once I took some time to reason with a 48-year-old friend
who kept asking me for the opportunity to make 250G. I asked
him what he wanted to do with 250G, and he said he wanted a
house. I thought it was a considerable and important request.
For fun I asked him why a house, and he said at his age he
needed the security of a home. Again, I thought it was a mature 
and appropriate request, so I felt proud not having to deal
with all those who waste my time for the sake of acquisition
and power. As I have grown to like this individual, I asked him
why he wanted the security of a home. He said that whenever
he is at a gathering or with friends he feels incompetent be
cause society judges people based on their homes, fancy cars
and wealth.

Since I am the one to bring the dream, I decided to ask more
questions to ensure I would get his request right. I asked, “So
you want 250G because you want a home, and you want a
home because you want security, because security provides
you with accomplishment and confidence? Accomplishment
provides self-esteem when you are with others. Why is self
esteem so important to you?” He answered that self-esteem
clears all his troubles and gives him a peaceful and tranquil
mind, and that he is more productive and happy when he has
peace and tranquility. If that is the case, and all you need is
‘peace and tranquility, why didn’t you ask straight away for
peace and tranquility? It would have been easier and less hassle. 
He didn’t think it could be achieved without the material
attachments.

Almost always I find people are not the best judges of what is
good for them. You need to look to history to search for the
hows and whys of life. You need advice, discipline and education 
to be the best judge for yourself. To choose well and
decide properly, you must know yourself. I looked into the
crowd, randomly pointed to a few of them and said it baffles
me how some humans spend time reading so much that is irrelevant 
to their life. They ignore all the troubles the wise
men of antiquity went through in order to leave us that rich
legacy. Have you read carefully the writings and ideas of Russian 
literature? Have you read carefully the writings and ideas
of Benjamin Franklin? Do you know that the stores that sell
the books you buy are the same stores that sell books with the
writings and ideas of my children, prophets and messengers?
